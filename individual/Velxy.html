About

Velxy Studios, LLC is an interactive event and entertainment company based in Dubai, focused on bringing gaming culture into the real world. Originally established as a T-rated game development studio, Velxy evolved into a full-scale live event brand centered around immersive, community-driven experiences.

The company blends gaming, technology, and lifestyle into physical events that offer more than just entertainment—creating spaces where players, creators, and communities connect in real time.
