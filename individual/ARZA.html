About

ARZA, LLC is a hardware engineering company specializing in the design and manufacturing of high-performance desktops and laptops for gamers, developers, and creators. Operating from Taiwan, ARZA combines precision engineering with performance-focused design to deliver systems built for demanding workloads.

Each system is developed with a focus on efficiency, thermal balance, and long-term reliability—ensuring consistent performance under real-world conditions.
