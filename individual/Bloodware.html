About

Bloodware, Inc. is a multidisciplinary technology company focused on building high-performance, secure, and scalable systems across software engineering, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, game development, and hardware engineering.

The company develops tools, platforms, and experiences designed for developers, creators, gamers, and advanced users—emphasizing efficiency, control, and long-term reliability.

Background

Bloodware, Inc. was formerly known as Blood Gang, Inc., a community-driven development group recognized within the Roblox ecosystem for its work in modding, scripting, and game enhancement.

  • Custom game modifications and scripting systems
  • Performance and gameplay optimization tools
  • Collaborative development and shared resources
⚠️ Some legacy projects may no longer be actively maintained following the transition to Bloodware, Inc.
